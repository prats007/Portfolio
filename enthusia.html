Enthusia

Enthusia is the annual sports festival of VJTI. It hosts a variety of sports and all departments compete against each other. Since many matches are played parallely we decided that a android application which would help track the results of all the sports will be beneficial for the students. We pushed real time data with help of organizers so that students get the latest fixtures. We also pushed reminders and alerts for upcoming matches. The app also provided international sports news through popular news feeds. I was the Front-end designer for android application in a 3 person team.
