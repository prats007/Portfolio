Research

Interviews and Obseravtions

With rise in number of household with pets, the need for pet care also increased. By conducting user interviews and observations we found out that the users were facing lot of problems while taking care of their pet. The data was analyzed using affinity diagram and empathy maps.

Affinity Diagram

    Users problems

  1. "I dont want my pet to starve"
  2. "Pet boarding is not affordable"
  3. "I dont trust technology to take care of my pets"
  4. "I can't always rely of friends in case of pet emergency"
